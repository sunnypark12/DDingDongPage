Help Center

Getting Started

How do I find restaurants?

Press the home button to see nearby restaurants. You can filter by cuisine type using the buttons at the top of the screen. Use the filter button on the right to find specific promotions.

How do I claim a promotion?

Tap on a restaurant pin on the map, view their promotions, and tap 'Claim' on the coupons you want. The coupon will be saved in 'My Coupons' section.

Where are my coupons?

Access your claimed coupons in the Profile tab under 'My Coupons'. Here you'll find all your active, expired, and redeemed coupons.

Location Services

Why does DDingDong need my location?

We use your location to show you nearby restaurants and their promotions. This helps you find the best deals in your area.

How do I enable location services?

Go to your device's Settings > Privacy > Location Services > DDingDong, and select 'While Using' or 'Always Allow'.

Promotions & Coupons

What types of promotions are available?

We offer percentage discounts, fixed amount discounts, free items, and buy-one-get-one deals. Each restaurant can customize their promotions.

How do I use my coupon?

Show your unique coupon code to the restaurant staff when paying. Each code can only be used once and must be used before the expiration date.

What if my coupon doesn't work?

Ensure the coupon hasn't expired and hasn't been used before. If issues persist, contact the restaurant or reach out to our support team.

Account Management

How do I update my preferences?

Go to Profile > Settings to update your cuisine preferences, notification settings, and personal information.

Can I delete my account?

Yes, you can permanently delete your account by going to Profile > Settings and scrolling to the bottom. Tap 'Delete Account' and confirm your choice. Please note that this action cannot be undone and all your data, including claimed coupons and preferences, will be permanently removed.
