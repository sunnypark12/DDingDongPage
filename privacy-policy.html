DDINGDONG Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 20, 2025

DDingDong ("the App," "we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you access or use our App. By using our App, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

1. Overview of Our Services

DDingDong is a promotion platform that connects restaurants with customers, unlocking exclusive culinary experiences and offers.

Users sign up either as:

  • Managers, who can manage restaurant information, create or edit promotions, and access a dedicated dashboard.
  • Customers, who can discover nearby promotions based on their location and personal preferences (e.g., age, preferred cuisines).

To provide these features, our App requires user registration, location permissions for customers, in-app notifications, and relevant personal data to enable personalized promotions and a secure management environment for restaurant owners.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Data

  • Name (First & Last Name): Required for Managers and Customers during setup.
  • Email Address: Required for both Managers and Customers for authentication and verification.
  • Password: Required to secure your account. An internal password for the app is not required when using a Google account.
  • Age: Collected for Customers to enable potential age-based recommendations or broader data trends and analysis.
  • Preferred Cuisines: Collected from Customers to enable potential restaurant and promotion recommendations.
  • Survey Responses: Customers should answer brief surveys about restaurant services for service improvement.

2.1.1 Coupon-Related Data

  • Coupon claim history
  • Unique coupon codes
  • Coupon redemption status and timestamps

2.1.2 Restaurant Data

  • Restaurant location and coordinates
  • Restaurant operating hours
  • Cuisine types
  • Contact information (phone number, website)
  • Reservation URLs
  • Restaurant Photo URLs

2.1.3 Promotion Data

  • Types (Percentage Discounts, Fixed amount discounts, Free item offers, Buy-one-get-one deals)
  • Creation timestamp
  • Discount Amount
  • Activation Status
  • Promotion Name, Start Date, Expiration Date, Edit Time Stamp

2.2 System Data

  • User ID (UID generated by Firebase): A unique identifier assigned upon account creation.
  • Timestamps & Logs: Recorded when users perform certain actions (e.g., signup, login, adding promotions).
  • Role Data: Customer or Manager designation.

2.3 Location Data

Approximate or Precise Location (Customers Only): If you grant location permissions, we use your real-time location to show nearby restaurants and promotions. You can disable location sharing through your device settings, but certain features (e.g., location-based search or promotions) may be limited or unavailable.

2.3.1 Location Usage Details

Location data is used for:

  • Displaying nearby restaurants on the map interface
  • Calculating distances to restaurants
  • Filtering promotions by proximity
  • Providing navigation assistance to restaurant locations

You can adjust location permissions between "While Using" and "Never" in your device settings.

2.4 Automatically Collected Data

  • Firebase Analytics (if enabled): May gather anonymized app usage statistics (such as session duration, screen views) to help us improve user experience and app performance.
  • Email Verification Status: To ensure valid email addresses and prevent fraudulent or duplicate accounts.

2.4.1 Usage Analytics

We track:

  • Promotion view and claim rates
  • Restaurant search patterns
  • Coupon redemption success rates
  • User engagement with different cuisine types
  • App feature usage statistics

3. How We Collect Information

Direct User Input

  • Users manually enter their email, password, and additional information (e.g., name, age, cuisine preferences) during account registration or in profile settings.
  • Managers provide a restaurant code to claim or manage a restaurant's page.

Automated Collection

  • Firebase Services: Firebase Authentication and Firestore automatically store user IDs, timestamps, and verification statuses.
  • Location Services: Our App periodically checks your location if you have given permission, to find relevant nearby promotions.
  • System Information: We may collect basic system information (app version, OS) to troubleshoot errors and enhance app performance.

4. How We Use Your Information

4.1 Provide and Maintain Our Services

  • Authentication & Account Management: We use your email and password to create and secure your account, along with email verification to confirm your identity.
  • Role-Based Features:
    • Customers: View promotions, conduct surveys, and access a map showing nearby deals.
    • Managers: Manage restaurant information, promotions, and special offers.

4.1.1 Real-time Features

Our app includes real-time functionality for:

  • Active promotion monitoring
  • Coupon availability tracking
  • Restaurant status updates
  • Location-based restaurant discovery

4.2 Personalization

  • Promotion Recommendations: We tailor promotions based on your location, age, and preferred cuisines.
  • Survey Feedback: Aggregate survey data to improve user experience and better understand customer preferences.

4.3 Service Improvements & Analytics

  • Usage Data: We analyze anonymized or aggregated usage data to identify trends, measure engagement, and improve the App's features and performance.
  • App Enhancement: Error logs and device info help us debug issues, optimize performance, and refine the user interface.

4.4 Security & Fraud Prevention

  • Account Verification: Email verification helps us reduce spam, multiple registrations, or abusive activities.
  • Policy Enforcement: We may use the data to enforce our Terms of Service, prevent fraud, or address any disputes that arise.

4.5 Automated Processing

Our system automatically processes:

  • Coupon generation and validation
  • Promotion availability and expiration
  • Restaurant distance calculations
  • User-restaurant matching based on preferences

5. Legal Basis for Processing

Depending on your jurisdiction, our legal grounds for processing personal information may include:

  • Consent: By creating an account and providing your details, you consent to this Privacy Policy.
  • Contractual Necessity: Some information (e.g., email, password) is needed to deliver our services (user authentication, manager dashboards).
  • Legitimate Interests: Ensuring app security, improving user experience, and aggregating survey results are in our legitimate interests.
  • Legal Obligations: We may process or disclose data where required by law or to comply with valid legal processes.

6. How We Share Your Information

6.1 Third-Party Service Providers

We rely on Firebase (Google Cloud Platform) for:

  • Firebase Authentication: Securely manage user sign-ups, logins, and email verifications.
  • Firestore Database: Storing user data (e.g., profile details, promotion settings, manager details).
  • Google Sign-In SDK: If you opt to sign up or log in via your Google account.

6.1.1 Firebase Data Structure

We store data in the following collections:

  • users: Personal account information
  • restaurants: Restaurant details and settings
  • promotions: Active and past promotional offers
  • coupon templates: Promotion templates and availability
  • user coupons: Individual claimed coupons
  • surveys: Customer feedback and preferences

6.2 External Links

  • OpenTable or Other Booking Sites: If a restaurant includes a link to an external reservation or coupon site, you may be redirected outside our App. We are not responsible for the content or privacy policies of external sites.
  • Location/Map Services: We may use Apple's or Google's map services for location-based features, subject to their respective terms of use.

6.3 Legal Compliance and Security

Law Enforcement Requests: We may disclose data if required by law (e.g., court orders, subpoenas) or when necessary to protect our rights or the safety of users, employees, or the public.

We do not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

7. Data Retention and Security

7.1 Retention Period

We retain personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide our services. If you delete your account, we will remove or anonymize your information from our systems, except where we need to retain specific records for legal, auditing, or compliance reasons.

7.2 Security Measures

  • Encryption (HTTPS/SSL): All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted.
  • Password Protection: Account login is safeguarded by Firebase Authentication.
  • Access Controls: Role-based access ensures that only managers can alter restaurant data.
  • Regular Updates: We keep our software and third-party libraries up-to-date to mitigate potential vulnerabilities.

7.2.1 Data Validation

We implement various validation measures:

  • Email verification for new accounts
  • Restaurant manager verification
  • Coupon code validation
  • Promotion redemption verification

8. Your Rights and Choices

8.1 Access and Correction

You can view and edit your profile information (e.g., email, age, cuisine preferences) in-app.

8.2 Account Deletion

You may delete your account at any time through the account settings or by contacting us. Once your account is deleted, we will remove your personal data from our active databases, though we may retain anonymized data for analytical or recordkeeping purposes.

8.2.1 Data Deletion Process

When you delete your account:

  • Personal profile information is removed
  • Claimed but unredeemed coupons are invalidated
  • Survey responses are anonymized
  • Restaurant management access is revoked (for manager accounts)

Note: Some transaction records may be retained for legal and business purposes.

8.3 Location Sharing

You can disable location permissions in your device's settings. Certain location-based features (e.g., local promotions map) may no longer function if you opt out.

8.4 Surveys and Preferences

Participation in surveys is optional. You may skip or decline to provide answers to any survey questions.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are strictly not intended for children under the age of 13 (or the minimum age required by your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect or store personal information from children under 13. If you become aware that a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately. We will take swift action to delete the information from our records.

Firebase may store or process your data in data centers located in the United States or other countries where Google operates. By using our App, you agree that your personal information may be transferred to and processed in locations outside your country of residence. These transfers are conducted in accordance with Google's compliance with recognized international data protection frameworks, ensuring a level of protection consistent with applicable laws. If you access our App from outside the United States, you acknowledge that your use of the App and any associated services is subject to this Privacy Policy, and your data may be subject to different privacy rights and protections than those in your country of origin.
