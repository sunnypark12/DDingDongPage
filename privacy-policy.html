1. Overview of Our Services

DDingDong is a promotion platform that connects restaurants with customers, unlocking exclusive culinary experiences and offers.

Users sign up either as:

  • Managers, who can manage restaurant information, create or edit promotions, and access a dedicated dashboard.
  • Customers, who can discover nearby promotions based on their location and personal preferences (e.g., age, preferred cuisines).
